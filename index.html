Our Motto

"Smart Education with Great Values"

GreenLand Public Higher Secondary School, Kotma is a Day cum Residential school located at Changeri, Kotma, Anuppur, Madhya Pradesh. It is an English medium, co-educational institution affiliated to the Madhya Pradesh Board of Secondary Education (MPBSE). The school is spread over acres of lush green land, away from the noise and pollution of the city.

Established in 2007, the school has consistently strived to provide quality education with a strong value system. We focus on the holistic development of every student — balancing academics, co-curricular activities, and sports. We are committed to helping children become socially aware and responsible global citizens.

Every classroom is equipped with smart boards and overhead projectors for audio-visual learning. Each child receives individual attention from our dedicated, trained, and experienced faculty. We offer online courses leveraging the latest educational technology, and fluency in English is a core focus for all students.
